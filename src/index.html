About Us

Our team is a young amateur team that often participates in tournaments with the strongest players in the world of Apex Legends. We constantly improve our skills and look at the development of the team to one day compete in the ALGS - the most prestigious and significant tournament in the world of Apex Legends. We are ready for hard work and open to learning in order to achieve our goals and win in this exciting game.


Our team consists of talented players who constantly work on improving their gaming skills and teamwork. We are close-knit and always support each other, as this is the only way to achieve high results in such a complex game like Apex Legends. We are focused on winning and ready to fight with the strongest teams to achieve our goals and become one of the best players in Apex Legends.
